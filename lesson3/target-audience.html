Name Target Audience Design

Description of the Target

  • Neighborhood business owners who wants to promote their businesses by increasing their sales and helping the local community.
  • Local residents who use the neighborhood's shops and look for good shopping opportunities (discounts) and seek entertainment and leisure options in the neighborhood.

Personas

Our purpose is to connect neighborhood traders to promote economic, social and cultural development. We work with the vision of being a relevant player in the growth and diversification of trade in our region, in order to contribute to our neighborhood becoming the largest commercial center in the city of São Paulo.
