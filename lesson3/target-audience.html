Target-Audience

Name Target Audience Design

Description of the Target

  • Neighborhood entrepreneurs who want to promote their brands, increase their sales and help the local community.



  • Local residents who use the neighborhood's shops and look for good shopping opportunities (discounts) and seek entertainment and leisure options in the neighborhood.

Personas

  • Merchant:

    • Person who owns a business in the neighborhood and wants his business to be publicized throughout the region and improve the vision of his brand for customers in the neighborhood.

  • Local Resident

    • Residents of the neighborhood who want to know what types of shops are around them, which ones have the best prices and/or what leisure and cultural options the neighborhood is offering at that time.

Scenarios

  • Are there any supermarkets, bakeries, gas stations, etc. in Pinheiros?
  • What leisure options does my neighborhood offer today or in the coming days?
  • Which stores in my region are currently offering promotions?
  • Which businesses in my neighborhood are involved in projects to take care of the environment in our region?
  • How can I get involved with social projects in my neighborhood?
  • How can I expose my brand to neighborhood customers?
